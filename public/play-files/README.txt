Place printable assets here.

Folder pattern:
/play-files/{category}/{slug}/preview.jpg
/play-files/{category}/{slug}/thumb.jpg
/play-files/{category}/{slug}/{slug}.pdf
/play-files/{category}/{slug}/{slug}.jpg

Example:
/play-files/coloring/farm-animals-coloring/preview.jpg
/play-files/coloring/farm-animals-coloring/farm-animals-coloring.pdf

After uploading real files, switch the slug to published in data/play.ts by adding the slug to the published Set inside defaultAssetStatus().
